George Carlin

Know about George Carlin

George Denis Patrick Carlin (May 12, 1937 - June 22, 2008) was an American stand-up comedian, actor, social critic, philosopher and author. He is widely regarded as one of the most important and influential stand-up comics of all time. He was once dubbed "The dean of counterculture comedians."

Some of his best stand ups:

  1. “On Location: George Carlin at USC” (1977)
  2. “George Carlin Again!” (1978)
  3. “Carlin on Campus” (1984)
  4. “Playin’ With Your Head” (1986)
  5. “What Am I Doing In New Jersey?” (1988)
  6. “Doin’ It Again” (1990)
  7. “Jammin’ In New York” (1992)
  8. “Back In Town” (1996)
  9. “You Are All Diseased” (1999)
  10. “It’s Bad For Ya” (2008)
